About Us

Right Foundation Childreen School, a bilingual nursery and primary school was established in year 2000 to provide nursery and basic education to children from ages 2 years(tender class) to 12 years(grade 6). The school is blessed with season professional teachers under the leadership of Mrs. Abigael Olajumoke a graduate of University of Lagos with Bachelor in Education(Guidance and Counselling)/Economics.

The school is located in a serene environment at 58/59, Alhaji Animashuan Street, off Omilani Busstop, Ijeshatedo, Surulere, Lagos. The school can boast of decent accommodation conducive for learning.
